The team was made up of Daniel Lee, Jason Wang and myself for this assignment.


The World

This world is home to a wizard who can control time and space. The planet was chosen for its remote and barren landscape; making it perfect due to the lack of noise and distractions. A singular pillar was placed on this world by the wizard to act as a guard tower as he maintained order to the universe. From there, all the other planets in the universe can be seen and navigated to through the powers of the wizard.


The Movement

For my wizard, I wanted to have a strong and direct form of movement in Labanotation. When directing, I described my character as having a confident and slightly aggressive walk with exaggerated arm movements. He was going to stand with his hands on his hips and his head held high since he’s a powerful being and nothing can hurt him. During the early takes, the movement, specifically the walk, looked too happy for the character I had in mind. So instead our actor Jason took a more aggressive approach to the walk and it turned out to be much better than how I imagined it. The final product was my wizard character stomping towards the edge of the tower and making an aggressive gesture before the video ends.
